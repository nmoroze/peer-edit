After spending hours working on a piece of writing, it can be hard to trust your own judgement. Peer editing can be a good way to revise your work, but is impossible without someone else. Enter Revisely, which allows you to post any piece of writing you are working on along with a specific question. By putting a point bounty on your question, you can ensure an accurate answer. Contributing to the community and answering others' questions will earn you even more points to ask questions of your own.

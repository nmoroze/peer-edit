Powerful Tools

Thanks to our unique annotation tools, Revisely is more suited for revising papers than a general question-and-answer site. Users can highlight snippets of text and annotate them with their feedback, allowing them to specifically pinpoint the parts of your submission that need work. Since all the notes are combined and made public, users can build off of each other's feedback to give you a comprehensive overview of what you need to improve.

Quality Feedback

With Revisely's unique point-based system, we ensure that all feedback given is relevant and high quality. You can reward points to the authors of good feedback, and give quality feedback so you have more points for your own papers. With the point economy, everyone on Revisely does their share of providing great feedback so that they can receive great feedback in return.
